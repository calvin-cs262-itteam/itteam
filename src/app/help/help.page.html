Easy Eats Help Menu Home Settings Help

Section 1: Search

You can use the Search feature of Easy Eats to find recipes quickly.

The following steps will help you search for a recipe:

1) Navigate to the Easy Eats homepage by clicking the menu icon (three horizontal white bars) and click on the button that says 'Home'.

2) Click on the white bar at the top of the homepage with the magnifying glass icon and grey placeholder text 'Find a new recipe!'.

3) Once the bar is clicked, type in the recipe you want to find and press the enter key to search.

4) Click the 'X' at the far right of the search bar to clear the text in the search bar.

Section 2: Viewing Recipe Steps

Easy Eats has a seperate page for each recipe that includes recipe steps.

The following steps will help you view recipe steps:

1) To view recipe steps, tap on the recipe to view the recipe's page. The recipe page displays steps below the recipe image.

2) On the recipe page, scroll down. You can continue scrolling down until all the steps have been viewed.

Section 3: Viewing Recipe Ingredients

Easy Eats has a seperate page for each recipe that includes recipe steps.

The following steps will help you view recipe ingredients:

1) Select a recipe from the home page or through the search bar. You should now be on the recipe page.

2) In the upper right corner you will see a grey basket icon. Click on this. The recipe ingredients will slide onto the screen.

3) To return to the instructions of the recipe, swipe right.

Section 4: Rate a Recipe

You can use the Rating feature of Easy Eats to share your opinion about certain recipes to other application users.

The following steps will help you rate a recipe:

1) Click on the recipe you want to rate.

2) Once you are on the recipe page, five greyed-out stars will be displayed near the top of the screen.

3) The stars are numbered 1 to 5 from left to right. A rating of one star indicates low satisfaction and a review of 5 star indicates high satisfaction. To submit your rating, click on the star that corresponds with the number of stars you would like to give. For example, clicking on star number three will give the recipe a rating of three stars.

Section 5: Viewing and Adding Suggestions

You can use the Suggestions feature of Easy Eats to provide alternate recipe steps for other application users.

The following steps will help you view and add suggestions to a recipe:

To view suggestions:

1) Click on a recipe where you want to place a suggestion.

2) Click on a step in a recipe where you want to place a suggestion.

3) All given suggestions for the step chosen will pop up. If none are shown then none have been given yet.

To add a suggestion:

1) View suggestions on the desired step.

2) Click in the popup box with placeholder text 'enter suggestion' and type your suggestion.

3) Click the 'Add Suggestion' button to submit your suggestion.

Section 6: Adding a Recipe

You can use the Add Recipe feature of Easy Eats to create a new recipe and add it to the application.

The following steps will help you add a new recipe to Easy Eats:

1) On the homepage, click the circular orange button with a plus sign on it. This will take you to a page where you can add a new recipe.

2) Type out the recipe name, tags, prep time, cook time, and servings in the boxes indicated on the page.

3) For ingredients, put in one ingredient per box. Click the ‘Add Ingredient’ button to add another ingredient, and click the circular button off to the side with a ‘x’ to delete an ingredient.

4) For recipe steps, put in one step per box. Click the ‘Add Step’ button to add another step, and click the circular button off to the side with a ‘x’ to delete a step.

5) Click the ‘Take Picture’ button to take a picture of your dish.

6) When finished, click the ‘Add Recipe’ button to submit your recipe.
